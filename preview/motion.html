MOTION · TIMINGS + EASING
Motion at Ajared is restrained. Most things should feel like a page turning, not a slot machine. Use snap for direct-manipulation; standard for navigational transitions; spring only for welcoming moments. The moiré pattern is the one exception — it drifts continuously as a living backdrop.
